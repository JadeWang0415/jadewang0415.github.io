About me


Contact me by jadewangy@163.com

I studied software engineering during my university in China, and got my master degree of engineering in France. I heard about Atelier Lumiere in 2018, the year when I back to China. I visited the exhibition of Van Gogh in 2019 in Paris and inspired by it.

I started learning digital art coding in 2021, and decided to focus on generative art in 2022 with my first work.

I am now a consultant at a supply chain consulting company, and I hope to become an artist in the near future.
