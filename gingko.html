In my hometown Dandong, Liaoning province, the resiential streets are lined with gingko trees. Those 100-year-old trees witnessed the change of times and the grwoth of generations. Every time I return to this small city and walk on the street under these ginko trees, meomries of the past are awakened.

This work is created from a prototype of the leaves of gingko trees, which contains 11 templates for backgrounds, and 4 templates for the color of leaves, displaying the color changes of leaves during the four seasons.
The backgroud is generated by flow field, simulating the feeling of light passing through the tree leaves, with three modes to control the shades of its colors. White is not included in the color templates but can be added by chance.

The leaf itself is created by "noise", with different parameters to control the numbers of its generation. The shadow of the leaf is created by accident during my test, I really like it and would like to keep it, it gives me a feeling of the light shines through layers of overlapping leaves.
