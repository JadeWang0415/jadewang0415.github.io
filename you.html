It starts from a simple shape, repeating the creation of petal "component" and changing its postions at each frame.

I am using "noise" during the generation of shapes to make a difference between petals. But I don't want to control the color of the different layers. By giving them a random value, it allows the petal to be any color, even black or white. I was surprised that each final result has a wonderful color at the end. It makes me to think about our life: your helpless, your happiness, your power make you irreplaceable. That's why I want to name the work as "YOU".

No matter what is your background, no matter what situation you were or you are, you have chance to blossom with your own style and your own color, to be unique and wonderful. You may not be satisfied with the present, but no one knows what will happen in your next journey, and what a beautiful flower you are drawing.


YOU

This is my first generative work created in April 2022, simulating the process of flowers blooming.

One day when walking in the garden, I was inspired by the flower and would like to record my feelings at that time in the form of "art".
